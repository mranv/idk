I'm Back - Anubhav Gain Returns to Blogging

By Anubhav Gain

Published 2024-06-26

Document Update Information
Last Updated: June 26, 2024
First Published: June 26, 2024

It's Been a While

Hello everyone! I'm Anubhav Gain, and I'm excited to announce my return to blogging after a significant hiatus. It's been exactly 908 days since my last post on December 31, 2021. The rapid evolution of technology, particularly in AI and cybersecurity, has been remarkable during this time.

When I wrote my last article about MacOS System Extensions, I had planned to follow up with a comprehensive year-end review for 2021. That post never materialized, but the experiences and learnings from the past two and a half years have been invaluable.

What I've Been Up To

During my time away from blogging, I've been deeply immersed in the cybersecurity landscape:

  • Security Engineering Excellence: Currently serving as a Security Engineer at Infopercept Consulting, where I lead the development of advanced XDR/OXDR platforms
  • Enterprise Consulting: Provided cybersecurity consulting services to Fortune 500 companies including JPMorgan Chase, Mastercard, and ANZ Banking Group
  • Technical Innovation: Developed cutting-edge security solutions using Rust, Python, and cloud-native technologies
  • Entrepreneurial Ventures: Founded TechAnv Consulting, delivering comprehensive security solutions to clients globally

Why I'm Back

The cybersecurity landscape has evolved dramatically, and I believe it's crucial to share knowledge and insights with the community. My return to blogging is driven by several factors:

  1. Knowledge Sharing: The cybersecurity community thrives on shared experiences and collaborative learning
  2. Technical Documentation: Many of the challenges I've solved deserve proper documentation for others facing similar issues
  3. Industry Evolution: The rapid changes in security threats and defense mechanisms need continuous discussion
  4. Community Building: Connecting with fellow security professionals and aspiring engineers

What's Coming Next

I'm planning to share insights on various topics that I've been working on:

  • XDR/OXDR Platform Development: Deep dives into building enterprise-grade security platforms
  • Security Automation: Practical approaches to automating security operations
  • Cloud Security Architecture: Best practices for securing cloud-native applications
  • Incident Response Stories: Real-world experiences from the trenches
  • Open Source Security Tools: Reviews and contributions to the security tooling ecosystem

Looking Forward

The past 908 days have been a journey of continuous learning and growth. From developing security solutions for major financial institutions to building my own consulting practice, every experience has added to my expertise.

I'm excited to reconnect with the community and share these experiences. Whether you're a seasoned security professional or just starting your journey in cybersecurity, I hope my upcoming posts will provide value and insights.

Stay Connected

I encourage you to:

  • Subscribe to the RSS feed to stay updated
  • Connect with me on LinkedIn
  • Check out my open-source projects on GitHub
  • Reach out via email at iamanubhavgain@gmail.com for collaboration opportunities

Here's to a new chapter of sharing, learning, and growing together in the cybersecurity space!

— Anubhav Gain
